What is the African Wildlife Foundation?

The African Wildlife Foundation is a non-profit charity that has been running for 61 years starting in 1961. AWF strives to they plan to help endangared animals have a thriving life in the wild again. They also thrive to protect the endangerd lands of africa they do even more than just animal and land proetecton, but there is too much amazing things that they do that I can't list it all. AWF Works in a variety of countries including: Botswana, Cameroon, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ethiopia, Kenya, Mozambique, Niger, Nigeria, Rwanda, South Africa, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.
